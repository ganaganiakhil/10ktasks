Upcoming New Hero Bike

Harley-Davidson X440:

The big highlight of the Harley-Davidson X440 is its all-new heart. 
      The 440cc engine has been developed from the ground up and its power and torque output are noteworthy at 25 bhp and 38 Nm,  respectively. 
    The construction of the engine is simple, with a 2-valve head and air-cooling. Being a long-stroke engine, peak torque is delivered at just 4,000 rpm, and this engine is super tractable, which means commuting speeds will be stress-free. 
    On the high-speed track, I managed to clock a top speed of 134 kmph with my 100 kg heft and that really impressive.
